About Embark

In January 2022, Embark became part of Lloyds Banking Group, one of the UK leading financial services groups. We believe that Lloyds Banking Group’s investment resources and customer knowledge combined with our speed of wealthtech innovation perfectly positions us to understand the developing digital needs of our clients and consumers.

Embark Group’s technology powers some of the UK’s largest retail financial services businesses, with a wide portfolio of white label partners including NatWest Group, Coutts, Standard Life, Nutmeg, Bestinvest, Charles Stanley, Moneyfarm, Wealthsimple and HUB Financial Solutions.
