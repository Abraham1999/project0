About Embark Group

Our services

Embark Group is a technology-driven, financial services business and one of the leading digital retirement solutions providers to the advised and institutional markets in the UK. We operate from eight locations throughout the country.

In April 2023, we were named a Wealth Tech 100 company for the fifth consecutive year. The Wealth Tech 100 highlights the most innovative WealthTech companies in the UK that are delivering transformative technology to asset managers, banks and financial advisers.

We set out to shake up the UK savings market with a digital-first approach, combining straight-through processing capabilities with technical excellence in retirement wrappers. By scaling our business through technical innovation, acquisitions and a relentless focus on delivering the best solutions for our clients, we grew assets rapidly, joining a select and influential group of startup platform brands that successfully disrupted the traditional savings market.

We continue to work closely with financial businesses to deliver their long-term savings and investment propositions to UK consumers using our platform, investments and pensions experience and we also offer fund research and consultancy services.

About Embark

In January 2022, Embark became part of Lloyds Banking Group, one of the UK leading financial services groups. We believe that Lloyds Banking Group’s investment resources and customer knowledge combined with our speed of wealthtech innovation perfectly positions us to understand the developing digital needs of our clients and consumers.

Embark Group’s technology powers some of the UK’s largest retail financial services businesses, with a wide portfolio of white label partners including NatWest Group, Coutts, Standard Life, Nutmeg, Bestinvest, Charles Stanley, Moneyfarm, Wealthsimple and HUB Financial Solutions.

We are WealthTech the way it should be. We are Embark.

  • Deep and Proven Experience – We bring a wealth of experience across banking, investment, pensions and insurance sectors having worked extensively with intermediary distribution partners and consumers of all levels of affluence.
  • Market-Leading Digital Technology – We’re constantly working behind the scenes to improve our technology and service ensuring that our solutions are built for the future.
  • Customer Outcome Focused – We place the security of our clients’ assets at the centre of our operations and develop new functionality to help them meet their goals in the ever-changing financial landscape. The delivery of strong and consistent customer outcomes is central to our approach, how we invest and run our businesses.
